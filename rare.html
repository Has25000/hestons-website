Arceus

Arceus is one of the most legendary and powerful Pokémon in existence. Known as "The Original One," it is said to have shaped the universe with its 1,000 arms. This Mythical Pokémon is incredibly rare and can change its type using different plates, making it one of the most versatile Pokémon in battle. The Arceus card is highly sought after by collectors due to its legendary status and stunning artwork. Its golden holographic finish and intricate design make it a true centerpiece for any Pokémon card collection.
